Summary

 Decomposition is a computational thinking technique 
 that involves breaking down a large problem into smaller,
 more manageable tasks. An Example of decomposition in real 
 lise is,

 Cleaning your house, you can break the task into smaller
 subtasks, like making your bed and hanging up your clothes,
 and create a to-do list.
